About Mechatronic Systems Engineering

Home to the School of Mechatronic Systems Engineering (MSE), SFU’s Surrey campus is the youngest of the three campuses, located in the heart of Surrey City Centre.The MSE program is unique to provide students with training in mechanical, electrical, software, and systems engineering. MSE attracts bright and talented students by providing exciting career prospects, ample research opportunities, and a well-rounded curriculum. Some of SFU’s most successful engineering student teams are based out of Surrey campus.

About Software Systems

Software Systems is an applied branch of computer science which puts the emphasizes upon creating efficient software as opposed to traditional theory studies. The program is project-based and team-oriented to model after the industry, further developing industry-like skills while building upon practical, hands-on experiences.
